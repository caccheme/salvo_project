Click on a cell to mark the beginning of a ship and click on a second cell to mark the end of that same ship. No ships can overlap or be placed diagonally. If you are not happy with a ship's placement, delete that ship and place it again.

Once you have place all your ships, you can click the 'submit' button that will appear.
